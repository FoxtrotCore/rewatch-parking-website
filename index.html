CyTube Rules

(Separate from Afterburner rules, see below)

The official rewatch segment starts at 10:00 PM CEST and ends when the second episode of CL is over.

  • Rulings by mods are the final word, don't waste your time arguing with them
  • Absolutely no political/controversial talk
  • Play nice: no excessive crude language, no targeting others, no racism, sexism, etc., no external drama
  • No aggressive self-promotion
  • Absolutely no discussion about downloading and/or ripping FTF's work

Afterburner Rules

(Restricted for ages 18 and up)

Afterburner is a more informal community event that's meant to wind down the rewatch for the week wherein the CyTube queue is opened and community members are allowed to request their own videos. Previous CyTube rules are also enforced a tiny bit less during this time. It formally starts when a mod signifies in chat and from then forward the following rules apply. Afterburner ends at the discretion of the active mod.

  • Rulings by mods are the final word, don't waste your time arguing with them
  • Still play nice: no excessive crude language, no targeting others, no racism, sexism, etc., no external drama
  • Political/controversial talk is allowed so long as it's civil
  • Limited self-promotion is allowed up to once a week, namely in the form of requesting a video about whatever project(s) you're involved in
  • Absolutely no discussion about downloading and/or ripping FTF's work
